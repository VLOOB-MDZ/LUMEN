THE FIRST WISH
“When I Can Hold a Star”
You were never the star I wished to own—
for stars were never meant for hands.
They belong to the endless sea of night,
where only patient dreamers understand.
I stand beneath your distant light,
with empty pockets and unfinished skies.
My hands hold nothing worth your future,
only constellations drawn inside my eyes.
The universe has taught me this:
every sun begins as scattered dust.
No galaxy is born in brilliance;
it is gravity that earns its trust.
So I will not ask you to descend
from the heavens you deserve.
Not while my orbit still trembles,
not while my soul has yet to learn.
Instead, I’ll gather fragments of tomorrow—
build them into something bright.
A name that echoes with purpose,
a heart that does not fear the night.
Perhaps one day, when my horizon
can answer yours with equal flame,
I will no longer reach for a distant star—
I will simply whisper your name.
And if the cosmos is kind,
our constellations may finally align.
Not because I chased the brightest light,
but because I became one of my own.
Until then, I remain beneath this sky,
grateful that you exist at all.
For hope is its own little universe,
and every universe begins…
with a single star that refuses to fall.
— the keeper of this sky

LUMEN {{ infoTitle }}
LUMEN is a shared night sky. Every star in it is a wish someone released — a hope, a prayer, a small promise to themselves.
HOW IT WORKS
Write a wish and release it — it becomes a star, placed gently in the sky. Sign it, or let the night keep your name. Travel the sky, open the stars of strangers, and wish upon the ones that move you: every wish received makes a star burn a little brighter.
WHAT WE BELIEVE
Hope is quieter than noise. There are no likes, no followers, no feeds — only a sky that fills, one small light at a time. Be gentle: behind every star is a person.
— made for everyone who still looks up
Last updated — July 2026
WHAT WE COLLECT
Only what you choose to release: the text of your wish and, if you add one, a signature. There are no accounts, no email addresses, no profiles. We do not collect your location, contacts, or identity.
WHAT IS PUBLIC
Wishes are public and anonymous by design. Anyone visiting the sky can read them. Please do not include full names, addresses, phone numbers, or anything that could identify you or someone else.
COOKIES & TRACKING
No advertising trackers, no analytics profiles, no third-party cookies. Your browser's local storage remembers small functional things only — such as which stars you have already wished upon.
MODERATION
Anyone can report a wish. Reported stars dim while a human reviews them; wishes that contain hate, spam, or personal data are removed. We may keep minimal moderation records to prevent abuse.
REMOVAL
Want a wish you released taken down? Message us on TikTok or Instagram (@vloob.inc) with the wish text and we will let that star rest. Requests are honored within 7 days.
CHANGES
If this policy changes, the date above changes with it. We will never quietly widen what we collect.
